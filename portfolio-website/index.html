Soccer aka Wishbone

Soccer, a Jack Russell terrier, was the star of the PBS tv series Wishbone (1995). Of course since I'm only 3 I never got to see the show actually ON PBS but my owner lets me watch the re-runs sometimes. He was one of over one hundred dogs that auditioned for the role, and was picked for being the most expressive. (Also the cutest in my not so humble opinion)
